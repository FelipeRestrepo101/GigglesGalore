Somtimes the unexpected happens

In 1994 Gideon Giggly was nothing more than a simple woodshop worker, freshly out of highschool with 5 siblings to look after he scoured for ways to put food on the table. With nothing more then his sibling smiles to keep him going an idea came to his head. With giggles the value of gold he felt the need to share them with the world, a world where one could shop for giggles to their hearts content.
